# **HR-NVC** - **H**igh **R**esolution **N**eural **V**ideo **C**oding

</div>

<br>

<div align="center">

This repository contains our series of works on **HR-NVC**.

</div>

<br>

---

<br>

#### **DVC-SHBMM**: Deep Video Compression with Scaled Hierarchical Bi-directional Motion Model, MM24
- Proposes **scaled hierarchical motion compression** with parameter-shared motion codecs.
- Introduces the concept of **trustworthy motion modeling** to characterize the effectiveness of reference information.
#### **HR-NVC**: High Resolution Neural Video Coding with Bi-directional Confidence-Guided Reference Information Modeling, CVPR26, Highlight
- Systematically organizes and models reference signals across both temporal directions, enabling interpretable and efficient B-frame compression.
- Presents **the first NVC evaluated on 4K-resolution videos**.
